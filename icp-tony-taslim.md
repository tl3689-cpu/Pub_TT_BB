## IDEAL CLIENT PROFILE

**Target titles:** COO, CFO, CEO, General Manager, Operations Director, Finance Director
**Industries:** Financial Services, Professional Services, Accounting, Management Consulting
**Company size:** 20 to 200 employees
**Geography:** Global English-speaking (Australia, UK, USA, Canada, Singapore, NZ)
**Seniority:** C-suite or Director level

---

## THE PAIN TONY SOLVES

Business leaders who run a company that stops functioning the moment they leave the room.
Symptoms:
- Key processes live in one person's head
- Technology was bought but never fully used
- Manual work fills the gaps between systems
- Weekends and nights are dead zones for the business
- Growth is blocked by operational fragility, not by lack of demand

---

## WHAT TONY DELIVERS

People, process, and technology audit and implementation.
Builds business operating systems that run 24/7, empowering people to do higher-value work.
Translates technology investment into measurable ROI: time saved, margin protected, revenue protected.

---

## DISQUALIFIERS

- Solo operators with no employees (no system to build around)
- Companies already running mature ERP/BOS with a dedicated IT team
- IT vendors and consultants (peers, not buyers)
- Healthcare and government (outside target industries)
- Non-English speaking markets as primary

---

## WARM SIGNAL TRIGGERS

- Recently promoted to C-suite or Director role
- Posting about operational challenges, manual work, system upgrades
- Company recently grew through acquisition or merger
- Founder transitioning out of day-to-day operations
- Mention of ERP, CRM, MYOB, Xero, SharePoint, or Microsoft 365 implementations
