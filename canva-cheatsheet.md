Create a premium, professionally designed cheatsheet graphic based on the following copy.

STYLE & QUALITY:
The design should feel like it was created by a world-class editorial and brand designer for a senior business advisory. Modern, minimal, premium, clean, highly readable, visually balanced. Understated authority, not hype. Avoid generic Canva templates. The final result should feel polished enough for an executive audience.

BRAND COLOURS (Efficient Business Solutions):
- Deep Teal: #0F6E56 (accents, the title underline, key numbers, dividers; not for body text)
- Signal Amber: #BA7517 (the single action — bottom CTA strip only; used once, never for body text)
- Ink Charcoal: #16302B (near-black body text and headings)
- Warm Paper: #F7F5EF (background)
- Slate Teal: #5E736B (secondary text, labels, sub-captions)
- Subtle paper-grid texture in the background, very low opacity

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headings: Playfair Display, tight tracking
- Body: Poppins, generous line height
- Numbers and labels: DM Mono for accent

DESIGN DIRECTION:
- Elegant grid-based layout
- Strong typography hierarchy, the title 3-4x the body size
- Sophisticated spacing and alignment, generous margins
- Minimal but impactful, no clutter
- Editorial-style composition, like a business briefing card
- Visually engaging without being decorative for its own sake

LAYOUT REQUIREMENTS:
- Strong title at the top, with a 4px Deep Teal underline (#0F6E56)
- Organise content into clear numbered sections, cards, or blocks
- Key numbers in Deep Teal; secondary text in Slate Teal
- Use visual hierarchy to guide the eye top-to-bottom
- Elegant Slate Teal dividers and subtle geometric shapes where appropriate
- Optimised for social, vertical 1080x1350 format
- Margins and spacing extremely polished

ICONS & ILLUSTRATIONS:
- Custom-style thin-line icons next to each numbered item, in Deep Teal or Ink Charcoal
- Cohesive, premium, modern, one consistent style throughout
- Blend visuals naturally into the composition, no random decoration

TEXT STYLING:
- Important keywords in bold, never in colour (the teal accent carries emphasis)
- Varying font sizes for hierarchy
- Excellent readability on mobile
- Avoid overcrowding text, leave breathing room

OUTPUT STYLE:
The final design should feel like a mix of:
- Apple-style minimalism
- Modern startup brand book
- Premium editorial infographic (Monocle, The Economist briefings)
- High-end save-worthy LinkedIn reference card
- Composed, credible advisory aesthetic

OPTIONAL ENHANCEMENTS:
- Soft shadows under cards
- Subtle texture overlays on the background
- Thin-line illustrations
- Premium UI-inspired elements (corner brackets, fine rules)

FORMAT:
- Vertical, 1080x1350 (LinkedIn-optimal)
- High resolution, crisp typography, perfect alignment and spacing

BOTTOM CTA STRIP:
- Add a thin Signal Amber strip at the bottom (#BA7517) with the author's name + handle in Ink Charcoal text
- Keep the strip thin (8-12% of total height) so it does not dominate — this is the single action moment

## Tips for the best output

1. **Run the prompt as-is the first time.** Canva AI gets it right ~70% of the time on the first pass.
2. **If the design is too busy:** ask Canva "make it more minimal, remove decorative elements, more whitespace."
3. **If the amber strip is overpowering:** ask Canva "reduce the amber to the bottom strip only, keep body text Ink Charcoal."
4. **If icons are missing:** ask Canva "add thin-line icons next to each numbered item, consistent style, in Deep Teal."
5. **Save as a Canva template** the first time. Every future cheatsheet uses the same template, just swap the copy.
